Methodology

My name is Roman Rogowski. I've been climbing for over a decade, and I have a passion for breaking down and analyzing the attitudes, behaviors, and movements of climbers.

One of the greater joys I've experienced along my climbing journey is witnessing the moment a climber takes a step towards overcoming the "impossible."

I believe such moments can be cultivated via the practice of deep self-inquiry. To improve as climbers—to ask more of ourselves—we must ask more of ourselves.

As your coach, I'll help you learn how to analyze various aspects of your performance, how to ask yourself better questions, and how to discover your own unique solutions.

Through this approach, I believe we can overcome any limitations that are standing in your way of progress. Let's do this.

To book your initial consultation or virtual session, check my calendar to find a date and time when you are available to meet.

FAQ

Can I reschedule a booking?

Yes. When you confirm your booking, you'll receive a Google Calendar invite. Once you accept the invite, it will create a shared event on your personal calendar.

All you need to do is move the shared event, and I will be notified that you've rescheduled. That's it.

Please note that if you reschedule less than 24 hours in advance, it's possible that I might not be able to meet at the newly proposed time.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, but please note that refunds are not available once a booking has been confirmed.

To cancel a booking, please send me an email with a short explanation of the reason for cancellation.
