Methodology

One of the great joys in my life is seeing the blend of excitement and disbelief on a climber's face when they perform a task they once thought was impossible.

I believe these magical moments can be cultivated via the practice of deep self-inquiry. To improve as climbers—to ask more of ourselves—we must ask more of ourselves.

As your coach, I'll help you learn how to analyze various aspects of your performance, to ask yourself better questions, and to discover your own unique solutions.

Through this approach, I believe we can overcome any limitations that are standing in your way of progress!
